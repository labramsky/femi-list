Femi-List

A feminist's list of inspirational women

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

A renowned Nigerian author, Chimamanda's work has made a global impact. Her 2012 talk We Should All Be Feminists started a worldwide conversation about modern feminism.

Malala Yousafzai

A Pakistani advocate for girls' education and the youngest Nobel Prize laureate. Malala's established the Malala Fund, a charity dedicated to giving every girl the opportunity to learn and lead.

Serena Williams

An American professional tennis player and advocate for gender equality, winning a record 23 Grand Slam singles titles in her career. Serena has revolutionized women’s tennis.

Frida Kahlo

A legendary Mexican artist that is remembered for her self-portraits, filled with passion and pain. With her trail-blazing feminist art, Frida remains an icon of female creativity today.
